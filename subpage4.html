Last semester I was in a Green Roof Ecology class. After learning about green roofs, we had to turn in a final project.
My group decided our project should be a video documentary showing the benefits of green roofs in NYC.
We interviewed different experts in the field and filmed green roofs around NYC.
The main location where we filmed B roll was the Brooklyn Grange green roof at the Brooklyn Navy Yard.
